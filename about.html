Our Story

From One Chair
to Two Thriving Clinics

Smile Craft was born in 2009 in a small clinic in Porur, Chennai — founded by Dr. Priya Ramesh with a single dental chair and an unwavering belief: that every person, regardless of age or income, deserves a healthy, confident smile.

Word spread fast. Neighbours referred neighbours. Families came back generation after generation. By 2013 we expanded within Porur, and in 2018 opened our second branch in Kolapakkam — now serving patients from Tambaram to Adyar.

Today, with a team of 12 specialist doctors and state-of-the-art digital X-ray and CAD/CAM technology, Smile Craft remains proudly rooted in Chennai — understanding the local community, speaking your language, and always putting your smile first.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Everything we do at Smile Craft flows from three beliefs that have guided us since day one in Chennai.

01
spa

Ahimsa — Pain-Free Care

Rooted in the principle of non-harm, we are deeply committed to anxiety-free, gentle dentistry. From numbing gels before every injection to calming clinic environments, your comfort is our highest priority.

02
handshake

Nambikkai — Trust

Nambikkai means trust in Tamil — and it is the foundation of every patient relationship we build. Honest diagnosis, transparent pricing, no upselling. We tell you what you need, not what generates the highest bill.

03
workspace_premium

Thirmai — Excellence

Thirmai means skill and excellence in Tamil. We invest constantly in advanced technology — digital X-rays, CAD/CAM crowns, laser dentistry — and in continuous training so our team delivers the best outcomes possible.
