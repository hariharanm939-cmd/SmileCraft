Our Story

From One Chair
to Two Thriving Clinics

Smile Craft Dental Clinic was founded in 2014 in Porur, Chennai — with a single dental chair and a clear conviction: every patient in Chennai deserves specialist-level dental care that is accessible, honest, and pain-free. Dr. Rishikumar A, BDS MDS, built the clinic around that belief.

Word spread quickly through Porur, Mugalivakkam, and Madanandapuram. Families referred friends. Patients returned for implants, braces, and root canal treatment — and brought their children for paediatric care. By 2018, Smile Craft opened its second branch in Kolapakkam, extending specialist dentistry to South Chennai communities.

Today, with 2 qualified specialists and full digital diagnostic capability — including digital X-rays, CAD/CAM same-day crowns, and laser dentistry — Smile Craft remains proudly independent, deeply rooted in Chennai, and focused on one outcome: your long-term oral health.

What We Stand For

Our Core Clinical Values

Three principles have guided every treatment decision at Smile Craft since the day we opened our doors in Porur, Chennai.

01
spa

PATIENT — Painless Dentistry

Rooted in the principle of non-harm, every clinical procedure at Smile Craft is designed to minimise discomfort. Topical anaesthetic precedes every injection. Our calm, distraction-free environments are engineered to reduce dental anxiety — particularly for children and first-time patients.

02
handshake

TRUST — Clinical Honesty

Nambikkai means trust in Tamil — and it governs every patient interaction at Smile Craft. We provide an honest diagnosis, a clear treatment plan, and fully transparent pricing before any procedure begins. We recommend only what is clinically necessary, never what maximises revenue.

03
workspace_premium

SKILLFUL — Clinical Excellence

Thirmai means skill and mastery in Tamil. We invest continuously in advanced dental technology — digital radiography, CAD/CAM same-day crowns, diode laser dentistry — and in ongoing specialist training. Our standard: every patient receives the same quality of care they would expect at a private hospital.
