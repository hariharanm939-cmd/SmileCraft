Teeth Whitening Procedure and Results

Reviewed by Dr. Rishikumar. A, BDS, MDS — Smile Craft Dental Clinic, Porur & Kolapakkam, Chennai. Everything you need to know about professional teeth whitening before you book your appointment.

Quick Answer

What to know before reading

Professional teeth whitening in Chennai can brighten your teeth by 4–8 shades in a single session. Laser whitening gives the fastest results. Dentist-prescribed home kits offer excellent gradual whitening over 10–14 days. Over-the-counter strips and charcoal powders are largely ineffective and can damage your enamel.

Why are your teeth not as white as they used to be?

Your teeth are not stained for one reason — they are stained for several, and the right treatment depends on which type you have. At Smile Craft Dental Clinic in Porur and Kolapakkam, this is the first thing Dr. Rishikumar checks before recommending any whitening treatment.

Surface stains (extrinsic) are caused by

  • check_circleFilter coffee and tea — the most common cause in Chennai patients
  • check_circleTobacco — cigarettes, beedis, and pan masala
  • check_circleRed wine, fruit juices, and fizzy drinks
  • check_circlePoor brushing habits and tartar buildup over time

Deep stains (intrinsic) come from

  • check_circleNatural ageing — enamel thins over decades, revealing the yellower dentine beneath
  • check_circleTetracycline antibiotics taken during childhood
  • check_circleExcess fluoride during tooth development (dental fluorosis)
  • check_circleTrauma — a knocked or injured tooth can darken over time

Surface stains respond very well to professional whitening. Deep intrinsic stains respond partially — in some cases, veneers or composite bonding give better final results. Dr. Rishikumar will assess your specific type of staining at your consultation.

Whitening options available at Smile Craft Dental Clinic

1. Laser Whitening — Fastest Results

A high-concentration hydrogen peroxide gel is applied to your teeth and activated with a laser or LED lamp. In about 60 minutes, teeth brighten by 6–8 shades. Best for patients preparing for a wedding, interview, or event who want maximum results in a single appointment. Results last 12–18 months with good maintenance.

2. Zoom Whitening — Popular Premium Option

Zoom whitening uses a dedicated lamp system to accelerate the whitening gel. Delivers 6–8 shades improvement in one session and is associated with slightly lower post-treatment sensitivity compared to standard laser whitening for some patients.

3. Dentist-Prescribed Home Whitening Kit

Custom-fitted trays and professional-grade whitening gel used at home for 1–2 hours daily over 10–14 days. Results are excellent — 4–6 shades improvement — and more gradual. Best for patients who prefer whitening at home or have mild tooth sensitivity.

4. Combined Treatment — Best Long-Term Results

One in-office laser session followed by a dentist home kit. This achieves up to 10 shades improvement with results lasting 2–3 years. Dr. Rishikumar recommends this most often for patients who want lasting change rather than a one-time treatment.

What results can you realistically expect?

Dr. Rishikumar believes in setting honest expectations before any treatment. Here is what patients at our Porur and Kolapakkam clinics typically experience:

  • check_circleLight tea and coffee staining — excellent response, 6–8 shades improvement predictable
  • check_circleModerate tobacco or ageing-related yellowing — good response, 4–6 shades
  • check_circleFluorosis or tetracycline staining — partial response, whitening alone may not be enough
  • check_circleCrowns, veneers, and bridges — these do NOT change colour with whitening gel
  • check_circleTeeth with active cavities or gum disease — whitening must wait until dental health is restored first

Do natural whitening methods actually work?

Patients ask Dr. Rishikumar about DIY whitening constantly. Here is the honest clinical answer on each method:

  • check_circleBaking soda — mild effect on surface stains only, abrasive with long-term use, occasional use only
  • check_circleActivated charcoal — no clinical evidence of whitening, may scratch enamel, not recommended
  • check_circleLemon juice — highly acidic, actively erodes enamel, avoid completely
  • check_circleOil pulling — no whitening effect but beneficial for gum health
  • check_circleWhitening toothpaste — mild improvement on surface stains, safe and useful for daily maintenance

How to keep your teeth white after treatment

  • check_circleBrush twice daily with a whitening-maintenance toothpaste
  • check_circleRinse your mouth with water immediately after coffee, tea, or wine
  • check_circleUse a straw for cold coloured drinks to reduce contact with teeth
  • check_circleProfessional cleaning every 6 months at our Porur or Kolapakkam clinic
  • check_circleUse your take-home trays once a month for ongoing maintenance
  • check_circleQuit tobacco completely — the fastest way to undo whitening results

Frequently Asked Questions

Is teeth whitening safe?

Yes. Professional teeth whitening at Smile Craft Dental Clinic is completely safe. Some patients experience mild, temporary sensitivity for 24–48 hours after treatment — this is normal and resolves on its own. Whitening is not recommended during pregnancy or for patients under 16.

How long does the treatment take?

In-office laser or Zoom whitening takes 60–90 minutes — a single appointment. Home kits take 10–14 days of daily use. The combined approach takes one in-clinic session plus two weeks at home for the best results.

Will whitening work on my crowns or veneers?

No. Whitening gel only affects natural tooth enamel. Crowns, veneers, and composite fillings will not change colour. If you have restorations on your front teeth, Dr. Rishikumar will advise you at the consultation to avoid mismatched shades.

Does teeth whitening damage enamel?

Professional whitening done correctly by a qualified dentist does not damage enamel. Temporary sensitivity is related to the whitening gel's interaction with dentinal tubules — not enamel damage — and resolves within 48 hours.

How long do the results last?

In-office whitening results last 12–24 months with good maintenance. Diet, tobacco use, and oral hygiene are the main factors that determine how long results last. Monthly at-home tray touch-ups significantly extend the results.
