Why Implants?

The Gold Standard in Tooth Replacement

Dental implants are a reliable tooth replacement option that supports jawbone health, improves chewing strength, and feels closer to a natural tooth than removable dentures.

looks_one
Looks Completely Natural
Your implant crown is custom-designed to match your natural teeth in colour, shape and alignment, ensuring a seamless and natural-looking smile.
fitness_center
Full Biting Force
Implants restore strong chewing ability for daily foods. They stay stable, helping you eat comfortably without slipping or movement.
hotel_class
Preserves Jawbone
A dental implant acts like a tooth root and helps reduce jawbone shrinkage that often happens after tooth loss.
all_inclusive
Permanent & Low Maintenance
Care is simple: brush, floss and attend regular dental check-ups. No removing, soaking or adhesives like dentures.
psychology
Restored Confidence
Patients often feel more confident speaking and smiling after implant treatment because the replacement tooth feels secure and natural.
savings
Cost-Effective Long Term
Implants are built for long-term use. With good hygiene and follow-ups, they can be a durable solution compared to frequent replacements.

Dental Implant Questions

Most healthy adults with good gum health are suitable for implants. Key factors include adequate bone volume, absence of uncontrolled gum disease and non-smoker status (or willingness to stop). We assess this thoroughly during consultation.

The procedure is performed under local anaesthesia, so you won't feel pain during surgery — just mild pressure. Post-operatively, discomfort is usually mild and well controlled with over-the-counter painkillers for 2–3 days.

With proper care, implant posts can last a lifetime. The porcelain crown on top typically lasts 15–25 years before replacement may be needed. Regular hygiene appointments significantly extend their lifespan.

Often yes — bone grafting can rebuild jawbone volume to make implant placement possible. We assess bone levels with a 3D scan and advise whether grafting is needed as part of your treatment plan.

Teeth in a Day (immediate loading) allows a temporary crown to be placed on the same day as implant surgery. This isn't suitable for every case, but when conditions allow it, you leave with a functional tooth the same day.
