With modern anaesthetics, getting a filling is virtually painless. You may feel slight pressure, but no sharp pain. We always take time to ensure you are fully numb before starting.

Composite (tooth-coloured) fillings typically last 7–10 years. Porcelain inlays can last 15–20 years. Longevity depends on oral hygiene, diet and the size of the restoration.

Root canal treatment has an undeserved reputation. With proper anaesthesia, it is no more uncomfortable than a filling. The goal is to relieve the pain caused by infection — not create it.
