About This Branch

Chennai's Most Trusted
Dental Destination

Established in 2012, our Anna Nagar branch was the first Smile Craft clinic in Chennai. Over 12 years, we've built a community of over 15,000 happy patients who trust us with their smiles. Our state-of-the-art facility spans 3 floors with 8 treatment suites, a digital radiology lab, and a dedicated children's zone.
