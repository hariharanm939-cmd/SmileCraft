Frequently Asked Questions

access_time

How quickly will Smile Craft reply to my enquiry?

WhatsApp messages are answered within 30 minutes during clinic hours (8 AM – 8 PM). Emails are responded to within 2 business hours. For the fastest response, use WhatsApp.

emergency

Do you offer emergency dental treatment in Porur?

Yes. Our Porur branch handles urgent dental cases — severe tooth pain, broken teeth, swelling, and root canal emergencies — including on Sundays. Call +91 8825745091 for immediate assistance.

credit_card

Does Smile Craft accept dental insurance in Chennai?

Yes. We accept major insurance policies including Star Health, ICICI Lombard, and New India Assurance at both branches. Share your policy details when you contact us and our team will verify coverage before your appointment.

translate

Can I communicate in Tamil at your dental clinic?

Absolutely. Our entire clinical and administrative team at both Porur and Kolapakkam speaks Tamil and English — ensuring comfortable, clear communication at every appointment.
